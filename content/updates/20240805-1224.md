Note that the function $s(x)=(\ln x) / x$ from the post has the following property:
$$
-s(e^{-x}) = -\frac{\ln(e^{-x})}{e^{-x}} = x e^x
$$
and the inverse function (branches) of $x \mapsto x e^x$ is commonly called the Lambert W function, which has undergone a lot of study.
