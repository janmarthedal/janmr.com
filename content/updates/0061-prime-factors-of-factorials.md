$$
d_p(n!) = \sum_{k=1}^\infty \left\lfloor \frac{n}{p^k} \right\rfloor = \sum_{k=1}^{\lfloor \log_p(n) \rfloor} \left\lfloor \frac{n}{p^k} \right\rfloor
$$
