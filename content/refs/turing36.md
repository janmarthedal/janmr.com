Introduces the concept of the Turing machine, a theoretical model of computation that formalizes the notion of an algorithm. Turing demonstrates that the Entscheidungsproblem (decision problem) is unsolvable by proving the existence of problems that no algorithm can decide. This paper laid the mathematical foundations of computer science.
